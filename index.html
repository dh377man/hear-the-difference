How to Play

  • 1. Tap ▶ Listen.
  • 2. You will hear a count-in: 1, 2, 3, Listen!
  • 3. Then you will hear Pattern A and then Pattern B.
  • 4. Tap ✓ SAME if the two patterns match, or ✗ DIFFERENT if they don't.
  • 5. After you answer, tap 🔁 Hear it again to listen one more time, then Next →.

There are 5 patterns in a round. Use headphones if you can!

Teacher Notes

Skill targeted: Aural discrimination of short tonal and rhythmic patterns — a foundational audiation skill.

  • Pitch Patterns: pentatonic (C–D–E–G–A); patterns grow from 3 to 6 notes; later levels add an octave leap.
  • Rhythm Patterns: steady tempo; patterns grow from 3 to 6 beats; later levels introduce eighth-note subdivisions and faster tempo.
  • Adapts automatically: advance after 4 of 5 correct; drops a level after 2 of 3 recent misses.
  • Stars: 5/5 = ⭐⭐⭐, 3–4/5 = ⭐⭐, 0–2/5 = ⭐. Progress saves to this device only.
  • No notation, no reading — purely aural. Headphones recommended in classroom settings.
